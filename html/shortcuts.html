V Scrolls to the video.
CTRL + > Skips to next episode.
CTRL + < Skips to previous episode.
ESC Scrolls to the top of the page.
C Displays the countdown.
1-9 Sets the countdown.
~ Clears the countdown.
Left arrow Right arrow Skips 5 seconds forward or backward in the episode.
M Mute
I Wont skip to next episode (for that video)
O Disable the intro skipping (for that video)
S Toggle synopsis
